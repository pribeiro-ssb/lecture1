IAAC IN THE MOON

Iaac students ready for the next step!

A group of worldwide students have jumped into the crazy adventure of overpopulate the moon in a
small crater with "just" 150km diameter. Together with SPACE X, IaaC will be preparing soon the first
tourist spacetrip to the new town in Petavius!
On Archdaily or Dezeen it is possible to find some of the most recent conceptual projects in the moon. Over the last years many studies have been brough to light by many architects.
